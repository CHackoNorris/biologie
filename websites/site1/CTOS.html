  • Power

    Power

    To guard against power outages, ctOS continuously monitors individual household power usage and is programmed to divert and shut down power in emergency situations.

  • Security

    Security

    A citywide CCTV network dynamically monitors all activity 24/7, from high-traffic city centers to suburban districts.

  • Transit

    Transit

    A sophisticated algorithm regulates traffic flow in concert with a network of citywide traffic signals and surveillance cameras.

  • Water

    Water

    The city’s water supply is tested continually for neutral ph levels and to detect foreign contamination.

  • Sewage

    Sewage

    Each household’s liquid waste is treated upon entry into the system. Sewage is disposed of and clean water is reintroduced all without human involvement.
